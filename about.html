About Us

Ringa Old Boys Association is an independent, non-profit organization that provides a platform for serving ROBIANs.

Know more

ESTD of This Alumni Association

Ringa Boys High School was started in the year 1966 with about 33 students who may have graduated in the year 1969.

 

Consequently, Ringa Boys High School may have produced high school graduates for about 50 years. From informal conversations among a few individuals, ROBA was established as a strong network of old students of Ringa Boys High School, who share common goals and are committed to holding high the torch of “Life is a Duty”.


Becoming a ROBIAN

ROBA is the central organization for registration of Ringa Old Boys’ High School past students, and is at the core of garnering support for the School. ROBA encourages all past students to register with it and also with other local and overseas chapters where applicable.

It is easy to become engaged in ROBA as an alumnus. You can attend an event organized by ROBA. You can volunteer to serve in ROBA. You can refer an alumnus to register as member of ROBA. You can read and pass along ROBA communications. You can wear ROBA gears wherever you are. You can speak proudly about ROBA and RInga Boys High School wherever you are. You can contribute a gift to Ringa Boys High School to address a need that you are passionate about. There are unlimited ways to engage with ROBA and Ringa Boys High School throughout your lifetime.

Membership in ROBA requires applicants to complete the Membership Form and submit a one-time membership fee of KSh.5000 which is due before getting membership number. As part of its accountability to members, ROBA will provide annual financial reports as well as periodic updates on projects undertaken.
